How You Can Help

Getting involved in your communities, or providing donations goes a long way in helping those in need.

Utah Department of Corrections

Offers a wide variety of outreach programs for familes and individuals in the system as well as volunteer oppurtunities to help those in your community.

Volunteer Programs
Iron County Care and Share

Organization that provides compassionate assistance and resources to individuals and families in need, creating opportunities for increased stability and self-sufficiency. Has food pantry, emergency shelter, and case-management services.

Website Donation Page
Volunteers of America: Utah

Program that provides shelter for those in need. Offers a plethora of services, as well as several ways to donate and volunteer to make a difference.
